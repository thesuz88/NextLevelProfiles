About Us

Next Level Profiles mission is to give your athlete a place to showcase their sports skills to the world.

What We do

We allow athletes to create their own sports profile, store all of their photos and videos in one place, organize your content, follow other athletes, and show your community who you really are.

All for Free.

Why we do it

Sports are special, and so is every athlete who participates in them. Athletics have the ability to bring out the best in people. We realize that ever athlete is different and has a story to be told.

Next Level Profiles is for all athletes to showcase their skills and show the world who they really are. There are too many stories untold of heroics, community service, volunteering and much more. Athletes can now tell their story here.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there an age restriction to sign up?

If an athlete is under the age of 13, they will need parental consent by agreeing to the terms and conditions. All other athletes with an age of 13 years or older are allowed to create a profile.

Do I have to part of a school organized team to have a profile?

No. While many athletes may be on a roster for a public, private or club sport, we want any athlete to have the ability to showcase their skills and personality. Athletes now have the ability to get discovered by other players, coaches and scouts even without being affiliated with an organized team.

What if I play multiple sports?

Athletes will be able to categorize their photos and videos by usting the '#' feature. When uploading your photos and videos, you will be able to put them into a specific cateogory like football, baseball, tennis, workouts, training, etc.

Can I only add pictures and video of sports related material?

Next Level Profiles is designed to be a platform for all athletes showcasing their athletic abilities and talents. With that being said, photos and videos uploaded to your profile or discover page should be intended for athletic use only. Examples include but are not limited to:

  • Off season training
  • In season training
  • Game footage
  • Workouts
  • Nutrition tips for performance
  • Sports boosters
  • Charity events

If pictures or videos are uploaded that do not have a intent on showcasing yourself in a positive way, they can be reported by viewers and taken down by Next Level Profiles

Can I use theis profile to help with recruitment?

Absolutely! For athletes looking to get recruited to play college athletics, we offer a free platform for you to display all of your skills and achivements.

Next Level Profiles is working on a "Pro" version specificly for players looking to get recruited that will offer more features to help your recruiting process.

What if someone creates a fake profile?

If you suspect a fake profile impersonating you or someone you know, you will have the ability to report their profile to our team and we will look into it. If we find the profile is impersonating someone else we will take it down.

Can other fans or athletes post pictures and videos of me?

Eventually, anyone will be able to upload a photo or video of an athlete with a profile by tagging the athlete.

Once the athlete is tagged, the athlete will get a notification asking if it is them and if they give permission for the photo or video to be posted publically.
